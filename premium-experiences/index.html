Premium Experiences

For the fan who wants an elevated experience at Orlando’s Signature Event. Our lineup of premium experiences offers all-inclusive opportunities for each round at the Arnold Palmer Invitational presented by Mastercard.

Premium experiences for your most valued guests.

Golf doesn’t get any better than this. Enjoy watching a field full of the PGA TOUR’s best while we take care of the rest. Each experience allows fans access to exclusive viewing areas complete with all-inclusive food and beverage service.

Our Elite Access Experience

Sit back, relax, and join the jet set. Paying tribute to Mr. Palmer’s aviator status and appreciation for the finer things, N1AP is a first-class experience — a one-way ticket to a side of the Arnold Palmer Invitational presented by Mastercard few will ever have the privilege of seeing.

Glide from frictionless parking to uplifting experiences, then soar well above cruising altitude while you watch the PGA TOUR’s best achieve greatness.

Wheels Up

Borrowing its name from the tail tag on Mr. Palmer’s cherished Cessna Citation X, N1AP is your passport to exclusive events and activations all tournament long. Enjoy tastings of fine spirits, unparalleled clubhouse access, VIP parking, and more.

N1AP Exclusive Experiences

Nautique Boating Experience

Enjoy a VIP demo ride experience aboard a Nautique Boat on the Butler Chain, a beautiful chain of lakes located steps away from Bay Hill Club & Lodge.

Player Appearances

Meet and mingle with PGA TOUR players without leaving the comforts of the Bay Hill Clubhouse.

Complementary Valet Parking

Begin each day with complimentary valet services at the Bay Hill Club & Lodge, where you’ll receive your N1AP credential to access the grounds, private viewing areas, and Member Lounge amenities.

Premium Experience

N1AP

The most elite way to experience API

A tail number says a lot about an aircraft, identifying its country of origin and more. In the case of the plane with N1AP on its tail, it signified that a legend was on board: Arnold Palmer. In the case of the Arnold Palmer Invitational presented by Mastercard, the moniker signifies the most elevated experience of our tournament, built on the spirit of the man behind “November-1-Alpha-Papa.”

Your All Inclusive Ticket Includes:

  • Exclusive clubhouse access, including private Members Lounge experience. N1AP guests also receive access to Orange Grove Bar & Patio and Arnie’s at the Orange Grove.
  • #18 – access to designated seating area on the legendary final hole at Bay Hill with covered seating, and complimentary bar service
  • #16 – exclusive covered seating area behind 16th Green with complimentary bar service​
  • CLUB 8 – daily access to CLUB 8 amenities off the 8th Green (non-reserved rows of seats), bar service and food complimentary​
  • Daily shuttle services from #16 to #18/Clubhouse
  • Valet parking daily at Bay Hill Club & Lodge​
  • Invitation to the API Tournament Week Event​
  • Luxury ”jet set” gift bag and more

Daily Access to Bay Hill

The NEW Orange Grove experience offers an upscale opportunity to entertain your most valued partners at the Arnold Palmer Invitational with premium access and exclusive offerings in the historic Bay Hill Clubhouse.​

ALL-INCLUSIVE Premium Amenities

With daily access to the storied Bay Hill Clubhouse and all-inclusive premium food and bar services, the Orange Grove is the Arnold Palmer Invitational at its sweetest.

ORANGE GROVE BAR & PATIO

The Orange Grove Bar features a variety of unique pub food offerings, featured items and cocktails; a TV lounge, and interactive experiences, with Arnold Palmer memorabilia on display. The Orange Grove patio, adjacent to the practice putting green, will feature bar service with outdoor seating and exclusive views as the PGA TOUR Players prepare for their round.

Private On-Course Seating

Access to a designated Orange Grove seating area on the beautiful ninth hole at Bay Hill with covered seating, and complimentary bar service. Enjoy the perks while the best golfers in the world compete just steps away.

Arnie’s at the Orange Grove

Located inside the Bay Hill Clubhouse, Arnie’s at the Orange Grove offers reserved table seating and food service daily during lunch hours in a private intimate setting.
